Jen

Founder, Hand in Hand

Jen has been traveling internationally for most of her life, with a primary focus on South Asia for the last 8 years. Those experiences —both good and bad— have shaped her convictions about partnering with under-resourced communities. She and her husband of 14 years both have a passion for global impact. Jen’s background in both marketing and the non-profit space serve her well as the Founder of Hand in Hand: A Global Collaboration of Courageous Women. She graduated from The University of Texas at Austin McCombs School of Business and also has an M.A.R. from Redeemer Seminary. She has worked in marketing, including owning her own marketing consultancy business, for many years, and has also ministered within church contexts, parachurch organizations, and in direct partnership with global NGO’s.
