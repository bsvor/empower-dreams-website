Who We Are

About Empower Dreams

A passion to see the body of Christ grow through effective partnerships.

Our Mission

Identify, Nurture, and Resource

Empower Dreams exists to identify, nurture, and resource highly effective discipleship and evangelism partnerships to grow the body of Christ.

We come alongside organizations that are already producing fruit and help take their results to the next level. Our focus is on making disciples in oral cultures and fostering Asian discipleship movements.

For over 10 years, we have built deep, personal relationships with our mission partners in Asia, Africa, South America, and beyond — visiting them regularly and ensuring every resource is prayerfully and wisely invested.

Our Values

What Sets Us Apart

01

100% Accountability

Every cent of your donation is applied to the project of your choice. The board of directors personally fund 100% of the organization's overhead expenses.

02

Personal Relationships

We build personal relationships with all partner organizations and personally visit projects to ensure best practices accountability.

03

Proven Partners

We come alongside organizations already producing fruit — pouring fuel on the fire to help take their results to the next level.

04

Culturally Compelling

We prioritize linguistically and culturally compelling approaches to discipleship, especially for the 70% of the world that are oral cultures.
