New ECFA Certification and Ready to Roll in 2019

We are proud to announce that Empower Dreams has received certification from the Evangelical Council for Financial Accountability (ECFA). This milestone affirms our deep commitment to integrity, transparency, and responsible stewardship of every dollar you entrust to us.

The ECFA is the gold standard for financial accountability among Christian nonprofit organizations. To earn this certification, Empower Dreams underwent a thorough review of our financial practices, governance, and operational integrity. The ECFA confirmed that we meet all seven of their rigorous standards, which cover areas including board governance, financial transparency, use of resources, and truthfulness in communications.

This certification is a direct reflection of our founding commitment: when you give to Empower Dreams, every cent of your donation goes to the project of your choice, because the board of directors personally funds 100% of overhead expenses. The ECFA certification gives you additional assurance that your generosity is being handled with the highest level of care and accountability.

As we step into 2019, we are excited about the opportunities ahead. We have ambitious plans to expand our reach, deepen our partnerships, and continue bringing the Gospel and practical support to communities around the world. With the ECFA seal of approval behind us and your faithful support before us, we are ready to roll into what promises to be our most impactful year yet. Thank you for trusting Empower Dreams with your giving.
