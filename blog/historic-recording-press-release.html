Historic Recording and Movies Press Release

Empower Dreams partners with Faith Comes By Hearing in a historic Audio New Testament recording and Gospel Films of the Books of Mark and Luke for a North African people group.

Faith Comes By Hearing (FCBH) in partnership with funding from Empower Dreams recently produced an Audio New Testament recording and Gospel Films of the Books of Mark and Luke for a North African people group. For security reasons, the language, people group and country cannot be revealed.

The historic recording in this highly remote area marks the first time these people have heard the entire New Testament or watched God’s Word in their own heart language.

“So faith comes from hearing, and hearing through the word of Christ.” — Romans 10:17

A Historic Achievement

This recording represents years of careful work, from translation to voice casting to production. The result is a high-quality audio experience that brings the New Testament to life in a language that has never before had access to Scripture in this format.

Gospel Films

In addition to the Audio New Testament, Gospel Films of the Books of Mark and Luke were produced. These visual presentations of Scripture are especially powerful for oral cultures, where storytelling and visual media are the primary means of communication.

The Impact

For a people group that has never had access to Scripture in their heart language, this is a watershed moment. The Audio New Testament and Gospel Films will be distributed throughout the region, reaching communities that have been isolated from the Gospel for generations.

Empower Dreams is honored to have funded this historic project. To God be the glory!
