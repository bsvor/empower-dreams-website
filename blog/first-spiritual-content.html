First Known Spiritual Content in Their Heart Language!

For the first time in recorded history, spiritual content has been created in a people group’s heart language — a breakthrough moment that opens the door to the gospel for an entire community.

Imagine living your entire life without ever hearing about God’s love in the language you think, dream, and feel in. For millions of people around the world, this is their reality. They may hear the gospel in a trade language or a national language, but it never fully reaches their heart because it is not in the language that speaks to their soul.

That is why this milestone is so significant. Through the faithful work of our partners, the first known spiritual content has been recorded in a people group’s heart language. This is not just a translation project — it is a door being opened for the very first time. The content was carefully crafted with cultural sensitivity, using storytelling methods and oral traditions that resonate deeply with the community.

The response has been powerful. When community members heard Scripture and spiritual teaching in their own language, many were moved to tears. For the first time, they could understand and internalize the message of the gospel in a way that connected with their identity and culture.

This is exactly the kind of work Empower Dreams exists to support — finding the most effective, culturally compelling ways to bring the good news to those who have never heard it. Your generosity made this historic moment possible, and we believe it is just the beginning of a great spiritual harvest among this people group.
