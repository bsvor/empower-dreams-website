Your Support is Bearing Fruit Across Nations

Empower Dreams partners are seeing lives transformed — from massive Jesus Film gatherings in Sudan to sustainable hope in Uganda's Katanga Ghetto. Here's how God is moving through Empower Dreams, thanks to your generosity.

A Heartfelt Thank You

Dear friends,

On behalf of everyone at Empower Dreams, thank you. Your prayers, fasting, and generous giving fuel Gospel breakthroughs around the world. Every gift, big or small, equips local leaders to share Christ's love in powerful, tangible ways.

This past week alone, we witnessed the ripple effect of your faithfulness. Let's celebrate what God is doing together.

A Powerful Week of Ministry in Africa

The Jesus Film: Audiences of 1,000+ and Counting

Your generous gifts through Empower Dreams equip our partners with powerful Gospel tools. God works through this Empower Dreams equipment as Pastor Ibrahim's team hosts Jesus Film showings for over 1,000 people at a time — and the response overwhelms us.

"Large numbers are committing to relationships with Jesus. I'm so thankful to see these fruitful examples of how our funding is being used." — Paul, after visiting the field

Hearts open. Lives surrender. People invite Jesus to reign as Lord and commit to long-term discipleship. These are not mere events; they are powerful moves of God. Empower Dreams stands honored and blessed to partner in what He is doing in East Africa.

Pastor Ibrahim is pictured with Empower Dreams Jesus Film and Turtle Box equipment.

Hope Rising in the Katanga Ghetto

Meet Wilson Namuwoza, our cherished partner in Uganda, and Ruth, a radiant woman of faith in the heart of Katanga Ghetto.

Ruth welcomes 17 souls — including three tiny infants — into her patchwork home no bigger than a large closet. Among them, several mothers fight to escape prostitution while scraping together meals for their children.

Yet God weaves beauty from brokenness.

A New Venture: Handmade Notebooks, Sustainable Futures

Ruth now teaches these women to craft and sell handmade notebooks. What started as a spark of hope becomes a thriving small business.

Wilson handed Ruth a microfinance loan to launch this venture. Every notebook sold means:

  • Food on the table
  • Children getting an education
  • Dignity restored
  • A future beyond survival

Join us in praying that this business succeeds financially and opens doors to redemption, healing, and lasting change.

On the Ground: How We Stay Connected

At Empower Dreams, we visit our partners regularly to:

  • See firsthand how God changes lives through your support
  • Encourage and strengthen our partners
  • Listen for what the Lord reveals about the future of their ministries

These visits ensure every resource is prayerfully and wisely invested.

100% Goes to Ministry

Our Board of Directors personally covers all administrative expenses. That means 100% of your donation goes directly to ministry work — no overhead, no waste.

Let's Keep Praying

  • For Pastor Ibrahim's team as they follow up with new believers
  • For Ruth and the women in Katanga — wisdom, provision, and open hearts
  • For every partner: strength, protection, and Holy Spirit boldness
