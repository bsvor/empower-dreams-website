Compelling Tools for Reaching the Unreached

Around the world, innovative and culturally compelling tools are being used to bring the gospel to people groups that have never had access to it before.

For centuries, the primary vehicle for sharing Scripture has been the printed word. But for the estimated two-thirds of the world’s population that lives in oral cultures — where knowledge is passed through stories, songs, and spoken tradition rather than text — a printed Bible is not the most effective tool. Reaching these communities requires a different approach.

Empower Dreams partners with organizations that are developing and deploying culturally compelling tools designed specifically for oral learners. Solar-powered audio Bibles deliver Scripture in heart languages without requiring electricity or literacy. Oral Bible storying methods train local storytellers to share biblical narratives in formats that mirror their own cultural traditions. Video and film projects bring the life of Jesus to life in languages and settings that feel familiar rather than foreign.

What makes these tools so effective is not just the technology — it is the cultural sensitivity behind them. When the gospel is presented in a way that respects and honors a community’s existing communication patterns, it removes barriers and allows the message to penetrate hearts. People are not being asked to adopt a foreign culture; they are hearing God speak to them in their own.

At Empower Dreams, we are committed to identifying and resourcing the most compelling tools available. Your partnership makes it possible to put these tools into the hands of faithful workers on the front lines of the Great Commission.
