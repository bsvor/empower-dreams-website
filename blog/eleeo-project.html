Eleeo Project — 10 Years of Partnering

For a full decade, Empower Dreams and the Eleeo Project have partnered together to bring the gospel to unreached communities. Here is a look back at what God has done through this faithful collaboration.

Ten years ago, Empower Dreams began a partnership with the Eleeo Project that would become one of the most enduring and fruitful collaborations in our history. The Eleeo Project is dedicated to reaching people who have never had the opportunity to hear the gospel, using creative and culturally sensitive approaches that honor the communities they serve.

Over the course of this decade-long partnership, the impact has been extraordinary. Indigenous leaders have been trained, communities have been engaged with Scripture in their heart languages, and lasting discipleship relationships have been formed. The Eleeo Project’s commitment to working through local leaders — rather than imposing outside structures — has been a key factor in the sustainability of the work.

What makes this partnership particularly special is the depth of the relationship. Empower Dreams has not simply been a funding source; we have walked alongside the Eleeo Project through challenges and celebrations alike. Regular communication, field visits, and prayer have knit our organizations together in a way that goes far beyond a transactional relationship.

As we celebrate ten years of partnership, we look forward with great anticipation to what the next decade will bring. The foundation has been laid, the fruit is evident, and the harvest continues to grow. Thank you to every donor who has made this partnership possible.
