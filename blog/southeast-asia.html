The Lord is Using You in Southeast Asia

Your faithful giving is bearing incredible fruit in Southeast Asia. Through our trusted local partners, discipleship movements are growing and the Gospel is reaching new communities.

Southeast Asia remains one of the most strategic regions for Gospel ministry. With enormous populations and diverse cultures, the need for culturally relevant discipleship is immense. Your support through Empower Dreams is making a real difference.

Growing Discipleship Movements

Our partners on the ground are training local leaders to multiply disciples in their communities. The approach is simple but powerful: equip faithful people who can teach others, just as Paul instructed Timothy.

Reaching Oral Cultures

Many communities in Southeast Asia are primarily oral learners. Through culturally compelling storytelling methods and audio resources, the Gospel is being shared in ways that resonate deeply with these communities.

The Lord is using your generosity to open doors that were previously closed. Thank you for your faithfulness in supporting this vital work.

To God be the glory!
