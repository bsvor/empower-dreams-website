2025 Year In Review

Your ongoing support has helped us continue spreading the gospel and making a difference worldwide! See how God worked through Empower Dreams in 2025 to bring hope, transformation, and lasting impact.

Audio Bibles

In 2025, Empower Dreams, alongside Faith Comes By Hearing, distributed thousands of Audio Bibles, empowering leaders to share God’s Word and fostering lasting relationships.

1,421 Total Audio Bibles Distributed
5 Countries Reached
25+ Languages

Distribution by Region

  • Peru — 200 Audio Bibles distributed in 7 languages
  • South Asia — 237 Audio Bibles distributed in 8 languages
  • Sudan — 300 Audio Bibles distributed in 5 languages
  • Tanzania — 30 Local Group Leaders Trained, 50 Leaders Awaiting Training
  • Uganda — 675 Audio Bibles distributed in 5 languages, 50 Local Group Leaders Trained

Peru

Our partners in Peru continued sharing the gospel through Audio Bibles in 7 different languages, reaching remote communities in the Andes and Amazon regions. These solar-powered devices bring Scripture to people who have never had access to a Bible in their heart language.

South Asia

In South Asia, 237 Audio Bibles were distributed across 8 languages. Despite significant challenges and persecution, discipleship movements continue to grow as believers gather in small groups to listen to and discuss God’s Word together.

Sudan

In Sudan, 300 Audio Bibles were distributed in 5 languages. The Jesus Film Backpack ministry continues to draw large crowds, with hundreds gathering for film showings and many responding to the gospel message.

Kenya & Tanzania

Our partners in East Africa trained 30 local group leaders and have 50 more leaders awaiting training. These indigenous leaders are the backbone of sustainable discipleship movements in their communities.

Uganda

Uganda saw the largest distribution with 675 Audio Bibles in 5 languages, along with 50 local group leaders trained. The Katanga Ghetto ministry continues to bring hope and transformation to one of Kampala’s most underserved communities.

All Nations — India & Romania

Our All Nations partners continued their work in India and Romania, training and sending workers into unreached communities. The Europe Multiply Team is building bridges for the gospel across the continent.

We are grateful for the successful distribution of 1,421 total Audio Bibles and the continued growth of discipleship movements around the world. Thank you for your faithful partnership!

Testimonies

Throughout 2025, we received powerful testimonies from Peru, South Asia, Sudan, Kenya, Tanzania, India, Romania, and Uganda. Lives are being transformed as people hear God’s Word in their heart language for the very first time.

Your generosity makes all of this possible. Every cent of your donation goes directly to these projects — the board of directors personally funds 100% of overhead expenses.
