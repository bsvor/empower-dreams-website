Fountain of Life Boys Home

The Fountain of Life Boys Home in Kenya continues to be a beacon of hope for vulnerable boys who have been orphaned, abandoned, or rescued from life on the streets. Here is an update on the incredible work happening at this home and the lives being transformed.

For the boys who call the Fountain of Life home, this place represents something many of them have never known: safety, stability, and unconditional love. Many of these boys came from the streets, where they faced hunger, exploitation, and despair on a daily basis. At the Fountain of Life, they are welcomed into a family where they receive nutritious meals, quality education, and most importantly, the knowledge that they are loved by God.

The home provides not only physical care but a comprehensive approach to nurturing each boy’s development. Education is a cornerstone of the program, with boys attending school and receiving tutoring to help them catch up and excel. Spiritual formation is woven into daily life through Bible study, prayer, and mentorship from caring staff who model what it means to follow Christ.

The results speak for themselves. Boys who arrived broken and hopeless are now thriving, excelling in school, and dreaming about futures they never thought possible. Some aspire to become teachers, doctors, or pastors—and with the foundation they are receiving at the Fountain of Life, those dreams are within reach. Your continued support makes this transformation possible, and we are deeply grateful for your partnership in this life-changing ministry.
