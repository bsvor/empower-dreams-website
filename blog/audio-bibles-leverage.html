Huge Discipleship Leverage with Audio Bibles

Solar-powered audio Bibles are creating extraordinary discipleship leverage in East Africa, with each device reaching 30 to 40 people per listening session in communities across Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda.

When we talk about leverage in ministry, we mean finding ways to multiply the impact of every dollar given. Few tools offer the kind of leverage we are seeing with solar-powered audio Bibles in East Africa. A single device, costing a fraction of what it takes to send a missionary, can reach 30 to 40 people in a single listening session — and those sessions happen again and again.

In Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda, these audio Bibles are being distributed to community leaders and pastors who gather their neighbors to listen together. The devices are solar-powered, so they work in remote villages with no electricity. They contain Scripture in local heart languages, so the message reaches people in the tongue they understand most deeply. And because the listening happens in a group setting, it naturally leads to discussion, questions, and discipleship.

The reports coming from the field are remarkable. Entire families and even whole villages are hearing the Word of God for the first time. Community facilitators are guiding listeners through the Scriptures, answering questions, and helping new believers take their first steps of faith. The communal nature of the listening sessions mirrors the oral traditions of these cultures, making the experience feel natural and inviting rather than foreign.

This is the kind of high-leverage, culturally compelling approach that Empower Dreams exists to support. Your gift toward audio Bibles does not just reach one person — it reaches dozens, and the ripple effects continue long after the device is delivered. Join us in putting more of these powerful tools into the hands of faithful leaders across East Africa.
