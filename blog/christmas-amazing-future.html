Christmas and the Amazing Future

As we celebrate the birth of our Savior this Christmas season, we are overwhelmed with gratitude for God’s faithfulness and the amazing future He is unfolding before us.

This year has been one of remarkable growth and deepening partnerships. From Peru to Kenya to Southeast Asia, we have watched God open doors that no one could shut. The generosity of our supporters has fueled projects that are transforming lives and communities in ways we never imagined possible when Empower Dreams first began.

Christmas reminds us that God entered the world in the most humble of circumstances — a manger in Bethlehem — and yet His arrival changed everything. In the same way, the seeds being planted through your faithful giving may seem small, but they are producing an eternal harvest. Audio Bibles reaching oral cultures, discipleship movements multiplying across nations, and orphans receiving love and care — these are the fruits of your partnership.

As we look ahead to 2023, we are more excited than ever. New partnerships are forming, existing ones are deepening, and the vision for reaching the unreached is sharper than it has ever been. We pray that this Christmas season fills your heart with the same joy and anticipation that fills ours.

From our family to yours, Merry Christmas and thank you for being part of the amazing future God is writing through Empower Dreams.
