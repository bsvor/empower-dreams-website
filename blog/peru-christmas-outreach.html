Peru Christmas Outreach

This Christmas season, our partners in Peru brought the joy and hope of Christ to children and families living in some of the most impoverished communities in the region. Through gifts, meals, and the sharing of the Gospel, lives were touched in powerful ways.

For many children in Peru’s poorest communities, Christmas can be just another day of struggle. Families living on the margins often cannot afford gifts, special meals, or celebrations of any kind. Our partners set out to change that by organizing Christmas outreach events that brought light and laughter to neighborhoods that rarely experience either.

Children received gifts and participated in activities that celebrated the true meaning of Christmas. Families gathered together for meals and heard the story of God’s greatest gift—His Son, Jesus Christ. The joy on the faces of these children and parents was unmistakable. For many, it was the first time they had ever received a Christmas gift, and the first time they had heard the message of salvation.

Your generosity made every gift, every meal, and every moment of this outreach possible. The impact goes far beyond a single day—seeds of faith were planted in hearts that will continue to grow. We praise God for the opportunity to share His love in Peru and thank you for making it happen through your faithful giving.
