Spring 2025 Thobo Update

See the incredible fruit being produced through our Thobo ministry partners in East Africa. Watch the latest video update on the progress happening in the Thobo region.

The Thobo region of South Sudan continues to experience extraordinary growth. Our partners on the ground, led by Pastor Ibrahim, are seeing the direct fruit of your faithful prayers and generous support.

What's Happening in Thobo

Since Empower Dreams began partnering with Pastor Ibrahim in the Thobo region, we've seen remarkable transformation. What started as emergency famine relief has opened doors to lasting spiritual impact.

Key Highlights

  • Churches continue to multiply across the Thobo area
  • New discipleship groups are forming in previously unreached villages
  • The Jesus Film backpack ministry draws hundreds to each showing
  • Local leaders are being trained to sustain the growing movement

Pastor Ibrahim and his team continue to demonstrate that when physical needs are met with the love of Christ, hearts are opened to the Gospel. The Thobo region is being transformed, and your partnership is making it possible.

Watch the video above for the latest update from the field, and join us in praising God for what He is doing in this region.

To God be the glory!
