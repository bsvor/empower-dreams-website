Fast Growing Discipleship

Something remarkable is happening in the communities where Empower Dreams partners serve. Discipleship movements are growing at an unprecedented pace, with new believers not only coming to faith but immediately turning around to share the Gospel with others.

The model is beautifully simple and deeply biblical: when someone encounters Jesus, they are trained to share their story and the Gospel with those closest to them. These new believers then disciple others, who in turn disciple still more. The result is an exponential multiplication of faith that far outpaces what any single missionary or organization could achieve on its own.

Across multiple regions where our partners work, we are seeing this pattern take root and flourish. Small groups are forming in homes, under trees, and in community gathering spaces. Believers who came to faith just months ago are now leading groups of their own, teaching Scripture and modeling a life of obedience to Christ. The growth is organic, Spirit-led, and deeply sustainable because it is driven by the people themselves rather than by outside resources.

This is the heart of the Great Commission in action. Jesus did not call us to simply make converts but to make disciples who make disciples. Through your support of Empower Dreams, you are fueling a movement that has the potential to reach entire people groups with the transforming power of the Gospel. We are humbled and awed by what God is doing, and we invite you to continue praying for and investing in this incredible work.
