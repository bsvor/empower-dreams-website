Year in Review December 21, 2019

Highlights — 2019

2019 was a year of tremendous growth and impact for Empower Dreams. From Kenya to Peru to Southeast Asia, your generosity fueled ministry that reached communities with the Gospel and tangible expressions of God’s love.

Among the highlights of 2019, we celebrated the expansion of discipleship movements that are multiplying rapidly across multiple regions. New believers are not only coming to faith but are being equipped to share the Gospel with others, creating a ripple effect that is reaching further than we ever imagined. The Maai Mahiu Ministry Summit brought leaders together in Kenya to cast vision and align efforts for even greater kingdom impact.

In Peru, our Christmas outreach brought joy and hope to children and families who live in some of the most impoverished communities in the region. In Kenya, the Fountain of Life Boys Home continued to provide a safe, loving environment for vulnerable boys who have nowhere else to turn. And across all our projects, the common thread was clear: God is at work, and He is using your faithfulness to accomplish extraordinary things.

As we close the chapter on 2019, we do so with hearts full of gratitude. Thank you for believing in this mission, for giving sacrificially, and for praying without ceasing. The seeds planted this year will bear fruit for generations to come. We look forward to an even greater 2020, trusting that the best is yet to come.
