Merry Christmas from Your Partner

As we gather with loved ones to celebrate the birth of Jesus, we pause to express our heartfelt gratitude to every partner who has made this year’s ministry possible.

Christmas is a time to reflect on the greatest gift ever given — God sending His Son into the world so that we might have life and have it abundantly. It is this gift that motivates everything we do at Empower Dreams. Every partnership, every project, every dollar stewarded is an extension of the love that first entered the world in a Bethlehem manger.

This year, your generosity has made a profound difference. Children in Kenya have been fed, clothed, and educated. Communities in Africa have received audio Bibles in their heart languages. Leaders in Asia have been trained to multiply discipleship movements. And through it all, the common thread has been love — the love of Christ working through the hands of His people.

We do not take your partnership for granted. You are not just donors; you are co-laborers in the most important work in the world. From our family to yours, we wish you a blessed and joyful Christmas. May the wonder of the season fill your hearts with peace and renewed purpose for the year ahead.

Merry Christmas,
The Empower Dreams Family
